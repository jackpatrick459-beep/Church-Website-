About Us

Our story. Our mission. Our people

Our Story

The roots of our faith were planted in 1906 when the first Adventist missionaries arrived at Gendia, Kendu Bay. From these humble shores, a movement of education and spiritual awakening began, led by pioneers like Dani Leah Onduru Oteko, one of the earliest converts whose courage helped establish missions from Gendia to Nyanchwa.

For decades, our community journeyed through challenges—from the inaugural Sabbaths at Oindo to the establishment of the Kanyamfwa station. This 120-year legacy of resilience culminated in the birth of the America SDA Church, inaugurated as a Sabbath School in 1975 and growing into the thriving District Headquarters it is today.

Our Vision

In harmony with Bible prophecies, the ultimate climax of history is the restoration of all things to full harmony with the divine will and righteousness. Guided by this perspective, the Church’s vision is to see every person having the opportunity to hear and accept the everlasting gospel before the return of Jesus.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who live as His loving witnesses and proclaim to all people the everlasting gospel of the Three Angels’ Messages in preparation for His soon return
